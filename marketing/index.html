Act I  ·  The company

Whether it's the school hall or the West End, your production deserves a proper stage manager.

Limelight is the app your company already wishes it had. Built for directors, teachers and cast who are tired of threaded emails, lost scripts and group chats that forgot to send the call sheet. Everything your show needs, tied neatly together and always in the right pocket.
