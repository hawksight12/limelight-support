Frequently asked questions

How do I sign in?

Use the email address your school, society or production company invited you with. If you have not received an invitation, please contact your director or admin.

I've forgotten my password

Tap "Forgot password" on the sign in screen to receive a reset email. If it does not arrive, check your spam folder or email us.

My rehearsal schedule is not showing

Schedules appear once your director has added you to a show. Pull down on the Schedule tab to refresh, or sign out and back in.

Audio tracks will not play

Make sure you have a network connection the first time you play a track. Once loaded, tracks are cached for offline use. Please also check your device volume and silent switch.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at dom@limelightlabs.co.uk from the address on your account and we will remove your data within seven days.
